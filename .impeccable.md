## Design Context

### Users
Primary users are admin-side operators managing the ARK Assured business dashboard. Mobile usage is possible for all users and should support quick monitoring and lightweight management actions.

### Brand Personality
Trustworthy, clear, and professional. The experience should feel reliable and efficient rather than decorative.

### Aesthetic Direction
Keep the existing visual language (navy, gold, clean cards) and improve usability through responsive layout adaptation. Prioritize balanced read-and-act workflows on mobile without removing core functionality.

### Design Principles
1. Preserve all core admin capabilities across screen sizes.
2. Use mobile-first responsive patterns: stack first, enhance for larger screens.
3. Prefer readability and tap comfort over density on small screens.
4. Maintain consistent brand styling while reducing friction in navigation and data views.
5. Keep interactions predictable and task-oriented.
